\section{Results}

The tables below are generated directly from the latest baseline, ablation, and student artifacts. Baseline rows establish the clarification benchmark, resampling ablations isolate which pieces of the new method matter, and the student table tracks how much of the teacher-wrapper gain can be recovered by modular post-training.
When a long-running phase has not finished yet, the corresponding table is intentionally rendered as a pending placeholder rather than omitted.

As of April 21, 2026, the strongest completed pivot-specific result is the InfoQuest development sweep. The first winning configuration, \texttt{k3\_r1\_c08}, improves task-success rate from \texttt{0.24} for \texttt{targeted\_clarify} to \texttt{0.29} for \texttt{resample\_clarify}, while preserving \texttt{1.00} appropriate-action rate. That result is sufficient to justify the fresh test-set gate, but it is not yet the final paper claim. The fresh \texttt{qwen3\_30b} test matrix, the selective-resampling ablation suite, and the student-distillation results are still in progress, so those sections remain explicit placeholders below.

\end{itemize}
