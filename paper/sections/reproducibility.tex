\section{Reproducibility}

Research runs record the resolved config, dataset hashes, model identifiers, prompt version, judge version, selected resampling hyperparameters, and repeatability summaries. Expensive phases are resumable at the shard level, job plans can be emitted for multiple terminals, and the end-to-end orchestration script writes a manifest tying together the baseline matrix, ablations, teacher rollouts, student training outputs, audit artifacts, and generated paper tables.

See \texttt{paper/experiment\_log.md} for the running narrative of experiment decisions and status.
